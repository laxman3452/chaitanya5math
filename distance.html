Example 1 (Multiplication): Multiply (22 cm 7 mm) by 5

Scenario: 5 ribbons of equal length are needed.

1. Multiply units individually:
    • 22 cm × 5 = 110 cm
    • 7 mm × 5 = 35 mm
2. Convert excess smaller units: 35 mm contains three full centimeters (30 mm = 3 cm) with 5 mm leftover.
3. Combine: 110 cm + 3 cm = 113 cm. Leftover = 5 mm.
Final Answer: 113 cm 5 mm (or 1 m 13 cm 5 mm)
Example 2 (Division): Divide (58 cm 8 mm) by 3

Scenario: Distributing a ribbon uniformly among 3 people.

1. Divide the centimeters first: 58 cm ÷ 3 = 19 cm with a remainder of 1 cm.
2. Convert the remaining unit: Convert the 1 cm remainder into millimeters → 1 cm = 10 mm.
3. Combine millimeters: 10 mm + 8 mm = 18 mm.
4. Divide the total millimeters: 18 mm ÷ 3 = 6 mm.
Final Answer: Each person gets 19 cm 6 mm
Example 5: Multiply (3 km 145 m) by 7
1. Multiply components individually:
    • 3 km × 7 = 21 km
    • 145 m × 7 = 1015 m
2. Handle values larger than conversion thresholds: Since 1000 m = 1 km, extract 1 km from 1015 m. Remaining meters = 15 m.
3. Add extracted kilometer up: 21 km + 1 km = 22 km.
Final Answer: 22 km 15 m
